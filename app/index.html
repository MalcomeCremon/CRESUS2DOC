What is CRESUS?

After conducting a study on the remuneration of freelancers we discovered that many of you, both experienced and inexperienced, were facing difficulties in setting their remuneration. Your remuneration depends on different criteria (experience, specialisation...). By adjusting your remuneration you optimize the performance of your missions.
